## 00_Synopsis: Moon Over Willows

**Novel Title**: *Moon Over Willows, A Rendezvous at Dusk*
**Author**: Nini

The world knows only of Li Qingzhao, but remains oblivious to Zhu Shuzhen, the other half of the Twin Jewels of Ci Poetry. 

Having happened upon Zhu Shuzhen's poetry and lamented her tragically sorrowful life, I was struck by the desire to help rewrite her fate. Thus, the character Qianli Xun was created—a female web novelist and the 508th-generation heir to the Qianli Fist. After enduring severe cyberbullying, she is caught in a massive earthquake when a Millennial Divine Willow suddenly uproots from the earth. Though Qianli Xun loses her life in the disaster, the divine power of the Millennial Divine Willow transmigrates her to the Song Dynasty, where she becomes Zhu Shuzhen. Thus begins her journey to alter destiny, living a completely different life on Zhu Shuzhen's behalf. 

This story is a work of pure fiction; history buffs, please do not take it too seriously! Literature is merely meant to entertain the soul.
