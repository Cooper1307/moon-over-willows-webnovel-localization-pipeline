## Chapter 2: Duel, Earthquake, and Transmigration

Before she could even figure out what to do next, her stomach started growling. A single glass of watered-down milk wasn't nearly enough. Qianli Xun walked into the dining room again and opened the fridge. Aside from milk, there was absolutely nothing.

She dialed her apprentice's number: "Hey, Er Gang, your master here is getting canceled on the internet. I can't leave the apartment; there's a huge mob of reporters swarming downstairs. Go buy me a box of rice noodle rolls, a cup of soy milk, and a bowl of spicy tofu pudding—and make sure the tofu pudding has extra chili."

"That's a lot. Can you even finish all that?" Er Gang asked.

"The whole internet is cursing me out. Shouldn't I eat my fill to deal with them?" Qianli Xun retorted. "Oh, right, when you come over, bring me an ancient-style women's outfit, and don't forget the veiled hat."

"What do you need those for?"

"I want to head back to Qianli Fist Manor. With so many reporters downstairs, I have to disguise myself so they won't recognize me."

"It's a good thing everyone only knows you're a web novel author and has no idea about your connection to Qianli Fist Manor," Er Gang replied. "Alright then, just head back to the manor and lay low. Wait for me, I'll be there soon."

About half an hour later, Er Gang arrived downstairs on his motorcycle. Carrying her breakfast and a large paper bag, he threw a glance at the reporters, walked into the building, whispered something to the security guard, and turned toward a side elevator. After waiting for a moment, the elevator doors automatically opened. He stepped inside and pressed the button for the 22nd floor.

The reporters didn't pay Er Gang any mind. Clad in a black martial arts uniform that exposed his muscular arms, he didn't seem the least bit connected to Qianli Xun.

Er Gang arrived at the 22nd floor and knocked on his master's door. After entering, he tossed the large paper bag onto the sofa, walked into the kitchen, and set down the breakfast on the dining table.

As he walked, he grumbled, "You really shouldn't have been writing novels, Master. Your status as the 508th generation successor of Qianli Fist is more than enough for you to flaunt, but now look—you're getting canceled by the whole damn internet. Oh well, from now on, you can just stay put at the manor and take on some commercial performances. You'll make enough money to live comfortably, and then you can marry yourself off. That's a perfect life right there."

"I am the master here! Since when is it an apprentice's turn to lecture his master?" Following behind Er Gang, Qianli Xun sat down at the dining table, opened the plastic bag, took out her breakfast, and started eating freely.

Er Gang said, "Take your time eating, Master. I'll wait for you outside the residential complex."

Qianli Xun didn't reply. She picked up a piece of the rice noodle roll with her chopsticks. Hearing the sound of the door closing as Er Gang left, she muttered to herself, "These apprentices of mine take me less and less seriously. When I get back to the manor, I am definitely going to discipline them."

Qianli Xun really did have quite an appetite. She polished off all the breakfast, wiped her mouth with a tissue, and tossed the crumpled paper into the trash can. She stretched lazily, then walked into the bathroom, where she brushed her teeth, washed her face, and tidied up her flowing long hair.

Once she finished getting ready, she returned to the living room, opened the paper bag, and took out the clothes and the veiled hat. Taking off her pajamas, she changed into the new outfit. *Not bad*, she thought. She looked just like an ancient swordswoman. She placed her phone and other valuables into her bag and slung it across her body. Finally, she put the veiled hat on her head. With the light gauze draped over her face, it would indeed be hard to recognize her.

She went to the entryway, changed into a pair of casual shoes, opened the door, and got into the elevator. When she reached the first floor, an inexplicable sense of nervousness gripped her. As she passed by the security guard, he was swiping through short videos on his phone. Judging by the audio drifting over, the videos were blasting Qianli Xun yet again. The guard paid no attention to the person heading out, continuing to watch the videos with immense interest.

Qianli Xun stepped out of the lobby doors. Through the translucent veil, she saw the reporters all looking her way, making her even more anxious.

A female reporter took the initiative to greet her: "Hello there, excuse me, which floor of this building do you live on?"

"The 22nd floor."

Hearing '22nd floor,' the reporters instantly swarmed around her. "You live on the same floor as Qianli Xun? Do you know anything about her situation?"

Qianli Xun immediately regretted not giving a different floor number. She could only blame her own extreme nervousness, which had caused her IQ to plummet.

"I don't know her very well. All I know is she lives opposite my unit," she lied smoothly. "Early this morning, I heard miserable crying coming from her place—probably frightened by all the cyberbullying. Anyway, you guys keep busy, I have things to do."

Qianli Xun frantically escaped from the crowd of reporters and reached the complex gates, muttering to herself, "Thank God they didn't recognize me."

"Master! Over here!" Er Gang's voice called out.

Qianli Xun rushed over, swung her leg over the back seat of the motorcycle, and wrapped her arms around Er Gang. Accompanied by the roaring engine of the motorcycle starting up, they were already speeding down the road, heading straight toward Qianli Fist Manor.

Qianli Fist Manor was built in the suburbs of the city. There were over a hundred apprentices, including both male and female disciples.

When the motorcycle came to a halt outside Qianli Fist Manor, Shen Jianchen, dressed impeccably in a suit and leather shoes, had once again brought his Shen Fist disciples to face off outside the manor's main gates. It looked like the two sides hadn't actually started fighting yet.

Qianli Xun took off her veiled hat, tossed it into Er Gang's arms, and shouted, "Shen Jianchen! Aren't we supposed to duel on the 25th of every month? Why are you here early?"

Shen Jianchen turned around. Seeing Qianli Xun dressed as a swordswoman, looking exceptionally beautiful, his deep eyes curved as he let out a soft chuckle: "I woke up early this morning and saw you become the focus of the entire internet, so I rushed over to check on you."

Er Gang hung the veiled hat on the motorcycle, walked up to Shen Jianchen, and teased, "You're the CEO of a huge corporation. Leaving your big company unattended and bringing your Shen Fist disciples to challenge us every month—could it be that you've taken a fancy to my master?"

Shen Jianchen stared fixedly at Qianli Xun: "It's true that I've taken a fancy to your master, but the duel is also real. You apprentices should spend some time persuading your master. She's already an old maid hitting thirty; if she doesn't get married soon, no one's going to want her."

After getting cyberbullied all morning with nowhere to vent her anger, hearing someone call her old was the last straw. Qianli Xun's fury boiled over: "Yours truly is only turning thirty tomorrow!..."

Shen Jianchen chuckled, "Stop calling yourself 'yours truly' all day long. You're already not that young, it only makes you sound even older."

Qianli Xun grew even more furious: "Shen Jianchen, listen to me. Yours truly is going to duel you to the death today. If you lose, you are never allowed to come to Qianli Fist Manor again!"

"And what if you lose?" Shen Jianchen challenged.

"If I lose, I lose. What are you going to do to me? Besides, yours truly will absolutely not lose!" Qianli Xun shot back.

"If you lose, you marry me," Shen Jianchen declared.

Hearing Shen Jianchen's words, the Qianli Fist disciples started whispering among themselves: "This CEO Shen is great in every way, except he has no idea how to court a girl. He's pissing Master off to this extent; how is she ever going to marry him?"

Qianli Xun retorted, "In your dreams! When pigs fly, yours truly will reconsider whether to marry you! Meet me tonight at seven by the Millennial Divine Willow. Come alone, you're not allowed to bring anyone else!"

"Got it!" Shen Jianchen looked extremely happy. He turned to his disciples and ordered, "Retreat!"

The Shen Fist disciples followed their master toward a Rolls-Royce. Seeing the constant smile hanging neatly on his face, one disciple asked curiously, "Master, you're about to get beaten up, why are you so happy?"

"What do you mean 'beaten up'? This is clearly a date! Tonight, when the bright moon rises by the Millennial Divine Willow... it reminds me of a poem." Shen Jianchen beamed, not worried in the slightest:

> *When the moon ascends the willow's crest,*
> *Lovers rendezvous at twilight's rest.*
> [TN: A famous line from the Song Dynasty implying a secret twilight rendezvous.]

The disciples watched him get into the car and murmured, "With a brain like his, I wonder how Master ever became a CEO? And how does he manage such a huge company? To even drive such a luxurious car?"

At dusk, beneath the Millennial Divine Willow in the suburbs, Qianli Xun wore her hair in a high ponytail. Dressed in a white T-shirt, a pair of casual jeans, and running shoes, she looked youthful and beautiful, not at all like a woman who was turning thirty tomorrow.

Shen Jianchen had changed into casual wear, looking relaxed yet incredibly handsome. He stared at Qianli Xun, his eyes brimming with affection.

The croaking of frogs from the nearby pond echoed in waves, breaking the silence between them.

Once the noisy frogs stopped, Shen Jianchen looked up at the moon. It was almost the fifteenth of the lunar month, and the moon hanging in the sky was approaching fullness. Looking back at the robust Divine Willow by the edge of the pond, he chuckled: "Hey! Qianli Xun, if you wanted to go on a date with me, just say so. Why use a duel as an excuse?"

"Which ear of yours heard that I wanted to date you? Yours truly is here to duel you!" Qianli Xun snapped.

As she spoke, Qianli Xun lunged forward like an arrow released from its bow, hurling a devastating punch straight at Shen Jianchen.

Shen Jianchen evaded swiftly to the side.

Unwilling to be outdone, Qianli Xun intensified her offense. She launched a fierce assault with a vicious combination of strikes, attempting to suppress her opponent.

Shen Jianchen dodged occasionally, parrying and deflecting her blows, and at other times neutralizing her offense with softer martial techniques. Seeing Qianli Xun unleash a flurry of rapid punches, he immediately channeled more force into his own fists and met her head-on. When their fists collided, both were sent staggering backward over two meters by the sheer concussive force.

"Qianli Xun, why do you have to push yourself so hard as a girl? Why don't you just marry me? I'm quite the catch—not only am I the 360th generation successor of Shen Fist, but I'm also the CEO of a huge corporation. You wouldn't find someone like me even if you searched with a lantern in broad daylight. So many girls want to marry me, yet I'm only interested in you. After you marry me, you won't need to write web novels anymore. I'll take care of you."

Perhaps because Shen Jianchen called her a "girl," Qianli Xun's mood improved significantly, and she even felt a bit touched. Yet, verbally, she remained as unrelenting as ever: "Don't even dream about it. I told you, unless pigs can fly, yours truly will never marry you. I am a woman of my word."

Right as her words fell, the ground beneath their feet suddenly began to heave violently. The amplitude was so massive that the two of them, affected by the powerful jolts, couldn't steady themselves at all and crashed to the ground. Watching the nearby buildings turn to rubble in an instant, they finally realized an earthquake had struck, truly experiencing how fragile humans were in the face of nature.

The Millennial Divine Willow swayed violently due to the earthquake. Uprooted from the earth, it was on the verge of collapsing—and Qianli Xun was right underneath it.

Noticing this, Shen Jianchen struggled to climb up from the tilted ground and dashed toward Qianli Xun. Just as he threw his body over hers to shield her, the massive Divine Willow crashed down, completely crushing them beneath its weight. Blood seeped into the churning soil.

About ten minutes later, everything fell quiet. The surroundings were a complete wasteland. Qianli Xun felt herself peeling away from her physical body, floating into the air. Seeing her own mangled corpse pinned beneath the Divine Willow, she felt an agonizing pain.

Then, she saw Shen Jianchen peeling away from his corpse as well. She screamed loudly, "Shen Jianchen! Shen Jianchen!" But the other party couldn't hear her voice at all.

When Shen Jianchen floated high enough, their eyes met. Qianli Xun could sense that Shen Jianchen was loudly calling her name. She tried desperately to respond, yet neither could hear the other's voice.

Suddenly, a surge of divine power erupted from the thick trunk of the Millennial Divine Willow, sweeping away the two souls floating in the air. Qianli Xun only felt her vision plunge into absolute darkness. She was terrified...
