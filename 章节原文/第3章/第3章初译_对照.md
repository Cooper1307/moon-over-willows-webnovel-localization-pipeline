第3章 穿越迷惑逢家暴
## Chapter 3: Encountering Domestic Violence in the Confusion of Transmigration

被神秘的力量牵引着，千里寻不敢睁开眼睛，速度之快，加上恐惧，她连用心体会的机会都没有，只觉脑中一片空白，直到那股力量狠狠地把她甩了出去……
Guided by a mysterious force, Qianli Xun didn't dare to open her eyes. The sheer speed, combined with her fear, robbed her of any chance to experience it mindfully. Her mind was a complete blank until the force flung her out...

她听到一个声音：“小姐，您醒醒，快醒醒啊！呜呜，姑爷，你该不会是把我家小姐打死了？呜呜……！”
She heard a voice: "My Lady, wake up, please wake up! *Sob*... Master Lin, you couldn't have beaten my Lady to death, could you? *Sob*...!"

千里寻在内心寻思着：“小姐？姑爷？该不会是做梦吧？不，我，千里寻，在地震中丧生，难道来到另一个世界？是死后的世界？会不会很可怕？”
*My Lady? The lord? Surely this is a dream—no. I, Qianli Xun, died in an earthquake. Could I have crossed into another world? Is this the realm of the dead? Would it be terrifying?*

她依然闭着眼，眼皮却能感受到周围光线不错，难道死后的世界也有光线？不如睁开眼瞧瞧。
She kept her eyes closed, yet her eyelids could sense a faint glow around her. *Could even the realm beyond death hold light? There was only one way to find out.*

千里寻努力睁开眼睛，看到一位身着古装的丫鬟蹲在自己身边哭泣。
Qianli Xun forced her lids apart and found a maid in ancient dress crouching at her side, weeping.

丫鬟看到小姐睁开眼睛，立刻转悲为喜：“谢天谢地，小姐您还活着。”
The moment her Lady stirred, the maid's grief gave way to joy. "Thank heavens, My Lady, you're still alive!"

千里寻问：“我是不是死了？这难道是死后的世界？你是谁？”
"Am I dead?" Qianli Xun asked. "Is this the world of the dead? Who are you?"

丫鬟强调道：“小姐，您没死，您还活着，我是您的丫鬟苦荞呀！”
"My Lady, you haven't died—you are very much alive," the maid insisted. "I am your maid, Kuqiao!"

“我的丫鬟？苦荞？”千里寻写过好几部穿越故事的网络小说，这桥段太像她在小说里写到的，“难道我穿越了？不是真的死亡？”
"My maid? Kuqiao?" Qianli Xun had penned quite a few transmigration web novels in her time, and this scenario felt pulled straight from her own pages. *Could I actually have crossed over? Was I never truly dead?*

想到这里，她来了精神，才发现自己躺在地上。苦荞扶她站起来，只觉额头十分疼痛，用手摸一下，手指上竟然沾了些血迹。千里寻从小到大何时被人伤过？刚要发作，想起地震时尸身在血泊中的情景，又能活过来已是恩赐，气也就消去不少。
At this thought, her spirits lifted. She found herself lying on the ground. Kuqiao helped her up. There was a sharp pain on her forehead, and upon touching it, she found blood on her fingers. When had Qianli Xun ever been injured in her life? She was on the verge of lashing out. Then the image flashed back: her own corpse, lying in a pool of blood during the earthquake. Getting another chance at life was gift enough; her anger ebbed considerably.

她站稳后，瞧到面前站着一男一女。男的大约二十来岁，长相一般，但身材魁梧，一看就是个练家子，目光中写着“不满”二字。旁边那女的打扮得花枝招展，溜着头发，一身娇媚姿态，很不屑地瞧着自己。
After steadying herself, she saw a man and a woman standing before her. The man appeared to be around twenty—average-looking, yet his burly physique marked him unmistakably as a trained fighter, his eyes carrying an air of displeasure. Beside him stood a woman decked out in gaudy finery, her hair smoothly combed, exuding a seductive charm as she regarded Qianli Xun with undisguised contempt.

他们都一身古装，千里寻搞不清楚那古装叫什么，毕竟她曾经属于另一个时代。她瞧瞧周围，虽然里面的布置与现代化相去甚远，可看上去这户人家家境不错。
They were both in ancient attire. Qianli Xun couldn't figure out what the clothing was called; after all, she belonged to another era. She looked around. Although the interior decoration was far from modern, it seemed that this family was quite wealthy.

她瞅瞅自己身上，也是一身古装，再摸摸头上，有发髻、金钗，看来是真的穿越了。
She glanced at herself and found she was also wearing ancient clothing. She ran a hand over her head and felt a hair bun pinned with a gold hairpin. *So I really have crossed over.*

千里寻把苦荞拉到一边：“你刚说你是我的丫鬟，名字叫苦荞？”
Qianli Xun pulled Kuqiao aside. "Did you just say you're my maid, and your name is Kuqiao?"

苦荞瞪着眼睛点点头：“小姐，您该不会被姑爷打傻了？怎么会问我这个问题？”
Kuqiao nodded with wide eyes. "My Lady, has Master Lin beaten you senseless? Why would you ask me this question?"

“苦荞，你告诉我，我是谁？”
"Kuqiao, tell me, who am I?"

“小姐，看来您真的被姑爷打傻了，连自己是谁都不知道。”
"My Lady, it seems Master Lin really has knocked the sense out of you, to not even know who you are."

苦荞没有急着回答，走到男人面前：“姑爷，朱府老爷怎么着也是临安知府，官职品阶在你之上，你竟然敢出手打我家小姐，小心我回朱府告诉老爷治你的罪！”
Kuqiao didn't rush to answer. She walked up to the man. "Master Lin, Master Zhu is at least the Prefect of Lin'an, a much higher ranking official than you. How dare you lay a hand on my Lady! Be careful, or I'll go back to Zhu Manor and tell the Old Master to punish you!"

面对言语威胁，男人并不害怕：“她嫁给林家，就是林家的人，我打她是在教育她，嫁出去的女儿泼出去的水，临安知府也没有权利干涉。”
Unfazed by her threat, the man replied calmly. "She married into the Lin family, so she belongs to our household now. Beating her is just my way of disciplining her. A married daughter is like water poured out; even the Prefect of Lin'an has no right to interfere."

千里寻越听越糊涂，越听越担心，什么嫁给林家？难道她穿越的这位已嫁给那男的？她顾不上其他，继续拉过苦荞：“快告诉我，我是谁？”
The more Qianli Xun listened, the more confused and uneasy she grew. *Married into the Lin family—could the woman she had crossed into already be wed to that man?* Ignoring everything else, she pulled Kuqiao aside again. "Tell me quickly, who am I?"

苦荞看着可怜的小姐，告诉她：“您叫朱淑真，是朱府的千金。”
Looking at her pitiful Lady, Kuqiao told her, "Your name is Zhu Shuzhen. You are the daughter of Zhu Manor."

“朱淑真？难道是宋代女词人朱淑真？”千里寻读过不少朱淑真的诗词，很是喜欢，大部分背得滚瓜烂熟，“现在是什么年代？”
*Zhu Shuzhen? Could it be Zhu Shuzhen, the female Ci poet of the Song Dynasty?* Qianli Xun had read many of Zhu Shuzhen's poems and loved them dearly—most of which she knew by heart. "What era is it now?"

男人旁边的女人用娇滴滴的声音说：“练兵官，看来你真的把她打傻了，竟然不知道自己是谁，连什么年代都不知道，我来告诉你。”
The woman beside the man said in a honeyed, affected voice, "Military Training Officer, it seems you really have battered her wits away. She doesn't even know who she is or what era it is. Let me tell you."

女人走到千里寻面前：“现在是大宋宣和四年。”
The woman walked up to Qianli Xun. "It is now the 4th Year of Xuanhe of the Great Song Dynasty."

千里寻心想：“大宋宣和四年，果真是宋代，看来我真的穿越成女词人朱淑真，我作为千里寻生活的那个时代，朱淑真留下的资料太少，生卒年不祥，到底是北宋还是南宋也搞不清楚，既然是宣和四年，看来是北宋了……”
*The 4th Year of Xuanhe of the Great Song—so it truly is the Song Dynasty.* The realization settled over Qianli Xun. *Then I have genuinely crossed into the body of Zhu Shuzhen, the female Ci poet herself. In my own era, so little was recorded about her: birth and death dates unknown, her period disputed between Northern and Southern Song alike. But Xuanhe... that would place her squarely in the Northern Song.*

女人又拉着腔调说：“朱家姑娘，你既嫁给练兵官，就应该好好尽妻子的义务，不是我说你，他是个血气方刚的男人，把你这么一个如花似玉的女子放在家里，看着却不能碰，当然生气，才会对你拳打脚踢。”
Drawing out her words, the woman added, "Young Miss of the Zhu family, since you've married the Military Training Officer, you should properly fulfill your duties as a wife. Not that I should be the one saying this—but he is a vigorous, hot-blooded man. Keeping a woman as lovely as a flower at home, only to look at but never touch—of course he'd get angry and strike you."

“什么？拳打脚踢？”
"What? Punching and kicking?"

千里寻看着眼前这个男人，苦荞称他为姑爷，这个女人又说朱家姑娘嫁给练兵官，看来他就是朱淑真嫁的丈夫。按照千里寻了解的资料，朱淑真嫁人时正值二十岁，岂不是穿越后自己直接年轻了将近十岁？
Qianli Xun looked at the man before her. Kuqiao had called him Master Lin, and this woman had said the Zhu family's daughter married the Military Training Officer. It seemed he was indeed the husband Zhu Shuzhen had married. According to what Qianli Xun had read, Zhu Shuzhen was exactly twenty years old at the time of her marriage. Didn't that mean she had suddenly become nearly ten years younger after crossing over?

刚有些高兴，又觉得不对。千里寻到三十岁都没谈过恋爱，穿越成朱淑真才二十岁就嫁人，努力保住的清白就这样付诸东流，而且还被眼前的男人拳打脚踢。
Just as she was feeling a bit happy, something felt wrong. Qianli Xun hadn't even been in a relationship until she was thirty. Crossing over into Zhu Shuzhen—who had married at merely twenty—meant the innocence she had worked so hard to preserve was flushed down the drain just like that. Moreover, she had been punched and kicked by the man standing right before her.

想到这里，千里寻走到男人跟前：“你是朱淑真的丈夫？叫什么名字？干什么的？”
At this thought, Qianli Xun walked up to the man. "You are Zhu Shuzhen's husband? What's your name? What do you do?"

男人只觉眼前的妻子被自己拳打脚踢，一把推到柱子跟前被撞晕后，醒来不仅看着怪怪的，说话的语气也比以前硬气许多。他充满怀疑，可亲眼目睹一切，并未发生异样，况且她的样子就是朱淑真，并非他人。为了保持大男子的形象，男人并未理朱淑真，头扭向一边。
All the man could tell was that after punching and shoving his wife into a pillar—knocking her out cold—she had woken up looking strange, speaking with a far harder edge than before. He was suspicious, yet having witnessed everything himself, nothing out of the ordinary had happened. Her face was unmistakably Zhu Shuzhen's, no one else's. To save face, he turned his head away and refused to acknowledge her.

女人过去解释道：“他就是你的丈夫，叫林觉，是鼎鼎大名的练兵官，负责临安所有军队的训练和演练，你能嫁给林练兵官，就偷着乐吧！”
The woman went over to explain, "He is your husband, Lin Jue. He is the illustrious Military Training Officer, in charge of training and drilling all the troops in Lin'an. You should quietly count yourself fortunate to have married Military Training Officer Lin!"

练兵官？千里寻寻思，据她了解的资料上说朱淑真嫁了一位小吏，她对这段婚姻十分不满，整日郁郁寡欢。瞧眼前这位练兵官，趾高气扬，根本没有把自己的妻子放在眼里，她千里寻都瞧不上，何况词坛双璧之一——朱淑真。
*Military Training Officer?* Qianli Xun pondered. What she had read stated that Zhu Shuzhen had married a minor official and was highly dissatisfied with the marriage, living in constant melancholy. Looking at the arrogant Military Training Officer before her, who didn't take his wife seriously in the slightest, even she, Qianli Xun, couldn't stand him—let alone Zhu Shuzhen, one of the Twin Jewels of Ci Poetry.
[TN: Twin Jewels of Ci Poetry refers to the two greatest female Ci poets of the Song Dynasty, Li Qingzhao and Zhu Shuzhen.]

千里寻决定要替朱淑真教训教训林觉，那就从眼前这个女人开始：“老娘既然嫁给他，就是他的正室，你又是从哪里冒出来的？难道是他娶的小妾？”
Qianli Xun decided to teach Lin Jue a lesson on behalf of Zhu Shuzhen, starting with the woman in front of her. "Since *I* married him, I am his legitimate wife. Where did you spring out from? Could you be a concubine he took in?"

林觉和那女人更觉讶异，朱淑真本柔柔弱弱，怎么一下子说话既硬气又粗鲁？
Lin Jue and the woman were even more astonished. Zhu Shuzhen had always been delicate and timid. How had she suddenly become so tough and rude?

苦荞赶紧解释：“小姐，她是姑爷从青楼带回来的，是春香楼的商妓，叫春金巧，姑爷经常把她带回家气您。今日就是因为又把她带回家，您依然懒得理姑爷，姑爷才对您拳打脚踢。”
Kuqiao hurriedly explained, "My Lady, she is the woman Master Lin brought back from the Qinglou. [TN: Qinglou, or pleasure quarters, were elite entertainment establishments in ancient China.] She is a Merchant Courtesan from Chunxiang Pavilion, named Chun Jinqiao. Master Lin often brings her home to provoke you. Today, it was precisely because he brought her home again—and you wouldn't give him the time of day—that Master Lin laid hands on you."

听到这里，千里寻气不打一处来，她最讨厌打女人的男人，而且还公然把青楼女子带到家里来。
Hearing this, Qianli Xun was seething. She hated men who beat women most of all—let alone one who openly brought a Qinglou woman home.

“林觉，你是不是男人？竟然因为青楼女子打老婆，有本事到院子里跟老娘打一架！”
"Lin Jue, are you even a man? To actually beat your wife over a Qinglou woman! If you have the guts, come to the courtyard and fight *me*!"

林觉心里犯嘀咕：“她怎么跟以前的朱淑真完全不同？”
Lin Jue grumbled inwardly. *Why is she completely different from the old Zhu Shuzhen?*

林觉认为眼前的朱淑真晕倒后醒来风格大变，也不可能长什么新本事，不过是失忆，忘了他有多厉害，不如再教训她一顿，让她长长记性，知道嫁为人妇该尽的义务还是要尽的。
Lin Jue believed that the Zhu Shuzhen before him had simply undergone some strange shift in demeanor after waking from her faint, and that it was impossible for her to have developed any new skills. She was merely suffering from amnesia, forgetting just how formidable he was. He might as well give her another thrashing to jog her memory, reminding her that a wife's duties must still be fulfilled.

“既如此，为夫就陪你练练，这次为夫不会把你打晕，最多教训一下你。”
"In that case, your husband will practice with you. This time, I won't knock you unconscious; I'll at most put you in your place."

大家来到院中，不一会儿林府的下人都围过来，他们很好奇，以前主君打夫人总是关了门在屋里，这次怎么闹到院子里来？
Everyone arrived in the courtyard. Soon, the servants of Lin Manor gathered around. They were very curious. In the past, the Master had always beaten his wife behind closed doors in their room. Why had the commotion moved to the courtyard this time?

林觉很不屑地说：“朱淑真，为夫让着你，你先来。”
Lin Jue said disdainfully, "Zhu Shuzhen, as your husband, I will let you have the advantage. You go first."

千里寻握紧拳头：“既然你让朱淑真先来，别怪老娘不客气。”说完，千里寻一拳打去。
Qianli Xun tightened her fists. "Since you let Zhu Shuzhen go first, don't blame *me* for being impolite." With that said, Qianli Xun threw a punch.

林觉没当回事，认为朱淑真本是娇弱之人，拳头能有什么力度，只要轻轻一推，她肯定倒下。
Lin Jue didn't take it seriously, believing Zhu Shuzhen was an inherently delicate person. What force could her fists possibly contain? With just a gentle push, she would surely fall.

他伸出手掌去推袭击而来的拳头，当手掌挨到拳头时，感受到强大的威力，不由心里一惊：“她怎么突然变得如此厉害？”
He extended his palm to push the incoming fist. But the moment his palm connected, he felt tremendous force and couldn't help but startle inwardly: *How did she suddenly become so powerful?*

还没反应过来，千里寻另一只拳头已打在林觉的眼睛上，立刻出现一片淤青。再上一脚，将林觉踢翻在地，顺势用一只腿压在林觉的身上，并将两只手固定住：“就这两下子还有脸当练兵官？苦荞！”
Before he could react, Qianli Xun's other fist had already struck Lin Jue squarely in the eye, instantly raising a bruise. She followed with a kick that sent him sprawling. Taking advantage of the momentum, she pressed her leg down on his torso and pinned both his arms to the ground. "With moves like these, you still have the nerve to call yourself a Military Training Officer? Kuqiao!"

苦荞见小姐变得厉害，诧异的同时，又觉甚好，以后就不怕被人欺负，干脆利落地答道：“在！”
Seeing how fierce her Lady had become, Kuqiao was struck with equal parts amazement and delight—she need never fear being bullied again. She answered crisply, "Here!"

“拿绳子来！”
"Bring a rope!"

很快苦荞拿来粗绳，递给小姐。
Soon, Kuqiao fetched a thick rope and handed it to her Lady.

千里寻接过，迅速把林觉绑起来，看到旁边站着两位下人：“你们两个过来，把他押到屋子里关起来，只许送水送饭，不准放他出来。”
Qianli Xun took it and swiftly bound Lin Jue's hands. Noticing two servants standing nearby, she ordered, "You two—escort him to a room and lock him in. You are only allowed to deliver his water and meals; do not let him out under any circumstances."

两个男仆很听话，把林觉扶起来。
The two male servants were very obedient and helped Lin Jue up.

林觉虽被绑，嘴上依然不饶人：“小心我到官府告你欺压夫君！”
Even though Lin Jue was tied up, his mouth remained unforgiving. "Careful, or I'll report you to the authorities for mistreating your husband!"

“我还想告你不守夫道！”
"And I'll sue *you* for violating a husband's way!"

“你不是朱淑真！”林觉终于说出心里犯嘀咕的话。
"You are not Zhu Shuzhen!" Lin Jue finally blurted out what had been nagging at him.

千里寻走到林觉跟前，在他耳边轻声说：“你最好认为我就是朱淑真，否则她就是被你家暴而亡，毕竟是条人命，告到官府那里，你是要吃官司的。”
Qianli Xun stepped close to Lin Jue and whispered in his ear: "You'd best accept that I am Zhu Shuzhen. Otherwise, she died from your domestic violence. A human life is a serious matter; take this to the authorities, and you will face a lawsuit."

林觉只能闭口不言，被下人带进一个屋子关起来。
Lin Jue had no choice but to fall silent; the servants led him away and locked him in a room.

千里寻看一眼春金巧，走过去，狠狠在她脸上扇了一巴掌：“这是替朱淑真教训你的，还不赶紧滚回青楼去！”
Qianli Xun cast a glance at Chun Jinqiao, walked over, and slapped her hard across the face. "That was on Zhu Shuzhen's behalf. Now scram back to your Qinglou!"

春金巧想要还回一掌，结果被千里寻挡住，她的力气很大，亏是吃定了，只能捂着被打疼的脸，边哭边扭着腰肢离开林府。
Chun Jinqiao tried to return the slap, but Qianli Xun blocked her with ease. Having clearly come out the worse, she could only press her stinging cheek and leave Lin Manor, crying as she swayed her hips on the way out.
