## 交付物二：清洁版终稿（Clean Final Copy）

Chapter 4: Settling In as Zhu Shuzhen

With everything handled, Qian Lixun felt a sharp pain in her forehead. "Ku Qiao, which room does your lady live in? Quickly take me there, I need a good rest."

Ku Qiao stepped forward to support her mistress. "Follow me, Miss. The room you live in is right in the back."

Qian Lixun did not refuse Ku Qiao's support; she was simply too exhausted. The terrifying scenes of the earthquake were still vivid in her mind, and the lingering fear brought by that mysterious power had yet to fade. Moreover, settling into her new identity as Zhu Shuzhen required time to adapt. Now that she had finished dealing with those two for Zhu Shuzhen, the sudden relief allowed exhaustion to naturally wash over her.

Ku Qiao supported her mistress as they turned toward the back. Qian Lixun looked up and saw a garden gate with the words "Zhulan Residence" written on it. Stepping into the courtyard, she found it planted everywhere with bamboo and orchids, perfectly matching the elegant name of the residence.

Entering the room, she was greeted by an antique and elegant atmosphere. The furniture was all crafted from rosewood. An exquisite vase and an incense burner sat on the table, adding a touch of freshness. The window curtains were made of delicate fabric, giving the room a sense of airiness, while the floral and bird patterns on them breathed life into the space. Calligraphy and paintings hung on the walls, and together with the scrolls on the desk, a strong scholarly atmosphere washed over her.

"The room Zhu Shuzhen lived in is truly not bad!" Qian Lixun said as she sat down on the bed.

"What are you saying? You make it sound as if you are not my mistress." Though Ku Qiao harbored suspicions, she refused to acknowledge them. She fetched a small wooden box, opened it, and used a clean handkerchief to dab some ointment from inside, gently applying it to the wound on her mistress's forehead. "This medicine is very effective. Within two days, the wound on your forehead will be completely healed, Miss."

After applying the ointment, Ku Qiao put the small wooden box away and stood obediently to the side.

Qian Lixun eyed Ku Qiao. "Come closer, I have something to tell you."

Ku Qiao approached cautiously.

Qian Lixun lowered her voice. "Actually, I am not your mistress. My real name is Qian Lixun. In my era, I encountered a massive earthquake, probably over a 7.5 magnitude. I lost my life in the earthquake, and my soul was swept away by a terrifying force. When I woke up, I had entered Zhu Shuzhen's body. Even now, I can hardly believe this is real. Based on my guess, your mistress had already died from Lin Jue's domestic violence."

Ku Qiao refused to admit it, kneeling on the ground. "You are my mistress. You were merely knocked out by Master Lin and lost your memory, that's all."

"You saw it yourself, I know martial arts. Can your mistress do that?"

"My mistress only knows how to write poetry and lyrics; she doesn't know any martial arts at all. But this appearance, this figure—it is unquestionably my mistress. Others will only see you as the daughter of the Zhu family."

Ku Qiao's words were true. Qian Lixun had already died in the earthquake. Heaven had granted her a second chance at life, allowing her to become Zhu Shuzhen. In this era, everyone else would only see her as Zhu Shuzhen.

"Get up. Why are you kneeling? Sigh, since I have become your mistress, I might as well embrace the role of Zhu Shuzhen obediently. It's just that I don't possess any of her memories. Furthermore, I know martial arts, and more importantly, my way of speaking is definitely different from your mistress's. It'll be easy for others to recognize the difference; didn't you hear Lin Jue say I wasn't Zhu Shuzhen?"

Ku Qiao thought for a moment and said, "Don't worry, Miss. Sometimes strange things like this happen in our countryside, too. A person suddenly falls ill, and after they recover, it's as if they've become a completely different person. In the countryside, they call this 'rebirth'. If anyone asks, we'll just tell them Master Lin knocked you unconscious, and when you woke up, you underwent a rebirth. Naturally, you would be different from before."

"Good! Let's go with that! Besides, it is the truth. By the way, let me ask you, why did Lin Jue beat your mistress?"

"Why are you still saying 'your mistress'? It is you, Miss!"

"Right, it's me. I am Zhu Shuzhen." Qian Lixun felt extremely unaccustomed to saying this, but she had resolved to play the role of Zhu Shuzhen dutifully. From now on, she would have to bury the name Qian Lixun deep in her heart.

Ku Qiao said, "When Lord Zhu married you off to Master Lin, you were extremely unwilling. That's because Master Lin isn't your type at all. He only knows how to train soldiers, he's vulgar, and he frequently comes home drunk. What you liked were men who could accompany you by moonlight amid the blossoms, composing verses together. Therefore, after marrying Master Lin, you have never consummated the marriage with him..."

Hearing that the marriage was unconsummated, Qian Lixun couldn't help but rejoice. "Excellent! My purity is finally preserved!"

"Precisely because you refused to consummate the marriage with him, and even threatened him with death to keep him away, he brought an escort home and committed those shameful acts right in front of your face. When he saw that you didn't mind at all, he grew even more furious and resorted to violence."

"So that's how it is. Why did Lord Zhu... no, my father, marry me off to someone I dislike?" Qian Lixun slowly began to accept that she was now Zhu Shuzhen.

Ku Qiao felt satisfied with her mistress's transformation. "You and Young Master Xu were childhood sweethearts, innocent childhood playmates. Unfortunately, the Xu family later declined and moved to an unknown place. Before he left, Young Master Xu promised that once he obtained an official rank through the imperial examinations, he would definitely come back to marry you. But you waited and waited, for a full five years, and Young Master Xu never came. Yet you were already twenty years old, considered a spinster. If you waited any longer, no one would want you. It just so happened that Master Lin came to propose marriage. Lord Zhu and Madam thought the Lin family was not bad, so they agreed to the match."

Qian Lixun sighed with emotion; no matter the era, she always ended up being an old maid. She thought this to herself, yet her lips refused to admit it. "How can a twenty-year-old be considered a spinster? In the era I came from, I was already thirty and still unmarried, yet I still had several people pursuing me."

Mentioning this, she thought of Shen Jianchen. He had also been swept away by that mysterious force. She wondered if he had also time-traveled to this era, hoping he was safe and sound.

Ku Qiao's eyes widened. "What? Unmarried at thirty? You know, in this era, a thirty-year-old woman is practically at the age of becoming a grandmother."

Qian Lixun laughed. "Becoming a grandmother at thirty? You must be joking!"

"How could I possibly lie to you, Miss? Besides, I prefer the current you."

"Why is that?"

"The former Miss was good, but she didn't speak much, spending all day writing poetry that I couldn't understand. She was also frail and weak, constantly bullied by Master Lin. Now it's different. You not only chat with me, but more importantly, you know martial arts. Hanging out with you from now on means I definitely won't be bullied by anyone."

Qian Lixun chuckled. "Your mistress is both thirsty and hungry. Quickly bring me something to eat and drink. Once I'm full, I need a good sleep."

"Right away! I'll go prepare it for you right now."

After Ku Qiao stepped out, Qian Lixun walked over to the bronze mirror. The reflection was blurry, completely incomparable to a glass mirror. Nevertheless, she could still see that the face in the mirror was entirely different from Qian Lixun's; she was simply Zhu Shuzhen now.

She sat down in front of the mirror, carefully admiring the face, which was both classical and exquisite. "I didn't expect Zhu Shuzhen to be such a beauty."

She spoke to the mirror, "Zhu Shuzhen, listen to me. From now on, you are me, and I am you. I will use your name and identity to live on in your era." Saying this, she removed her hair ornaments one by one.

Zhu Shuzhen's hair was incredibly thick and long, as black as ink. This hair quality, placed in Qian Lixun's era, would absolutely qualify her for shooting shampoo commercials.

Just as she was pondering, Ku Qiao brought in a tray of food. "Miss, the kitchen happened to have made your favorite Double-colored Lotus Seed Soup with Chicken and Wild Vegetable Buns. I've brought you some; you can have a little bite to hold you over first."

Qian Lixun couldn't hold back anymore and hurried to sit at the table. "The food in the Great Song Dynasty is really not bad! Although there are only two dishes, they look remarkably exquisite, especially this chicken..."

"Double-colored Lotus Seed Soup with Chicken," Ku Qiao repeated, noticing her mistress had momentarily forgotten the name.

"Right, Double-colored Lotus Seed Soup with Chicken. Just by the name, you can tell it's not easy to make. It probably requires several steps and quite a long stewing time."

"You are exactly right, Miss. It is quite troublesome. Please, enjoy it now."

Qian Lixun couldn't resist any longer and began wolfing it down in large bites. It was truly delicious.

Ku Qiao stood aside, watching her eat. Her table manners were far more uninhibited than the previous Miss. Recalling the imprisoned Master Lin, she said with unease, "Miss, Master Lin is shouting in the room, claiming that you assaulted your husband and threatening to take you to the magistrate's court. In my opinion, it might be better to just let him go, to save us from his endless shouting."

Qian Lixun took a sip of the soup and devoured a bun in quick succession, speaking as she ate, "It doesn't matter. Once I'm full, I'll gag his mouth. Then no one will have to hear him blabbering nonsense."

"But Master Lin is a military training officer, after all. Imprisoning a military training officer of Lin'an City... I'm afraid the authorities will punish you."

"Punish for what? This is a domestic matter, the authorities won't interfere. Besides, if the authorities really want to meddle, they should first look into how he committed domestic violence against your mistress."

Qian Lixun polished off the soup and buns completely, stood up, and said, "Although the soup is delicious, it doesn't quench my thirst well enough. Get me some tea. I'll go check on Lin Jue first, and it'll be ready to drink when I come back."

"Yes," Ku Qiao replied. Watching her mistress step out the door, she walked over to the tea table set with tea utensils and began preparing tea.

Qian Lixun arrived outside the room where Lin Jue was locked up. A servant stepped out carrying a tray and an empty bowl—evidently having just finished serving Lin Jue his meal. Seeing Qian Lixun approaching, the servant hurriedly bowed, "Madam."

Qian Lixun deeply disliked this form of address, but as she was now Zhu Shuzhen, she had no choice but to accept it. She asked curtly, "Is he behaving himself in there?"

How could a servant casually comment on whether the master of the house was behaving himself? The servant replied vaguely, "The lord is very uncomfortable being tied up by Madam."

"You may leave."

After the servant left, Qian Lixun pushed open the door and bypassed the folding wooden screen to see Lin Jue trussed up securely and lying on the bed. He was quite calm at the moment, no longer making a racket.

Hearing footsteps, Lin Jue sat up immediately. He was, after all, a man with some martial arts foundation; he managed to rise swiftly without even using his hands for support.

Seeing Zhu Shuzhen walking toward him—with no hair ornaments yet still exceedingly beautiful—he was struck by a feeling that was both familiar and strange. This woman used to only write poetry he couldn't comprehend, spending her days with a furrowed brow, full of melancholy and sentimentality. She appeared both pedantic and aloof, and deep down, she looked down on a military man like him. Why was it that after being beaten unconscious by him, she woke up completely transformed? Her brow was much more relaxed, her speaking voice had grown louder, and crucially, she knew martial arts—her fists were truly formidable.

Lin Jue decided to soften his stance first. "My wife, we are husband and wife after all. Tying up your husband will only make us a laughingstock if word gets out. You should untie me."

Qian Lixun placed her hands on her hips. "Who is your wife? Who said we were husband and wife? Let me tell you, the former Zhu Shuzhen was beaten to death by you! *I* am the reborn Zhu Shuzhen! Not only am I no longer as easy to bully as before, but I'm also going to make you obediently give me a letter of divorce!"

### 二、核心改写逻辑阐述

#### 【最重要改动】B2 — "只是朱淑真"误译为"truly"

- **原文**：`她只是朱淑真`
- **原译**：`she was truly Zhu Shuzhen`
- **终稿**：`she was simply Zhu Shuzhen now`
- **逻辑**："只是" = only/merely/simply，是认命式的客观陈述——"不过是朱淑真"。而"truly"（真正地）含主动认同与肯定，语义方向恰好相反。此处是千里寻第一次在铜镜前正式接受自己新身份的关键场景，"simply...now"同时保留了"只是"的被动感与"此刻起"的时间节点。

#### B1 — 章节标题

- **原译**：`Embracing the Role of Zhu Shuzhen`
- **终稿**：`Settling In as Zhu Shuzhen`
- **逻辑**："Embracing"含主动迎接、欢迎之意，与"老实当好"（无奈却坦然地接受、认认真真去做）的被动妥协语气不符。"Settling In"准确传递了"落地生根、安下心来"的认命过程，与本章千里寻放下自我身份的核心主题契合。

#### A2 — "胡乱喊叫"的两处误译

- **原因**："blindly shouting"是直译失效的典型——胡乱 ≠ blindly。"胡乱"= 随意、乱来、毫无章法，英文对应"endlessly / making a racket"。两处统一修正，消除反复出现的不自然表达。

#### C1 — 两小无猜术语强制修正

- 术语表：`两小无猜 = Innocent childhood playmates`
- "childhood"是语义核心（指明儿时相识），不可省略。属于最终防线扫描必须拦截的术语一致性问题。

### 三、剩余翻译风险提示

| 风险点 | 描述 | 建议处理方案 |
|--------|------|-------------|
| **"老娘"的语调** | 末段千里寻自称"老娘"（底层女性强势自称），译文用斜体 *I* 处理，基本传达强调感，但火辣痞气无完美英文对应。 | 可考虑"*This* lady"/"Your new Madam here"，或编者注说明。 |
| **"重生"双层语义** | 苦荞语境中"重生"是民间"大病后性格突变"，非现代网文穿越概念。目前加引号处理合理，但英文读者可能误读为字面复活。 | 若后续章节有混淆风险，可添加译者注区分两种"rebirth"含义。 |
| **"娘子"的软化层** | 林觉叫"娘子"时正在服软，"My wife"准确但偏中性，未捕捉撒娇示弱的亲昵感。 | 后续林觉进一步软化场景可视情考虑"my dear"增温。 |
| **衙门口语化** | "magistrate's court"正式感略重，与千里寻口语化语气有轻微落差。 | 若全书倾向口语化，后续可统一为"the local authorities"。 |
