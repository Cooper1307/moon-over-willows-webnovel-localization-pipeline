第5章 一见钟情难休妻
Chapter 5: Love at First Sight Hinders Divorce

林觉之所以会到朱府提亲，只因偶然一次参加临安知府朱晞颜，正是他现任的岳父大人，举办的赏花钓鱼宴时，与朱淑真有过一面之缘。自此，她的美貌就像刻在他的心里，挥之不去。
The reason Lin Jue had gone to the Zhu residence to propose marriage was purely due to a chance encounter. He had attended a Flower-Viewing and Fishing Banquet hosted by Zhu Xiyan, the Prefect of Lin'an and his current father-in-law, where he caught a passing glimpse of Zhu Shuzhen. From then on, her beauty was etched into his heart, impossible to shake off.

记得那是一年的春天，百花盛开。朱晞颜差人给族叔林子孝送来请帖，邀请他参加赏花钓鱼宴。林子孝的诗画虽谈不上震惊临安，但在临安城文人中也占有一席之地。而朱晞颜作为临安知府也是位好诗词歌赋的文人，要不怎么会把女儿培养成为女词人。
He recalled it was spring that year, with all kinds of flowers in full bloom. Zhu Xiyan had sent someone to deliver an invitation to his clan uncle, Lin Zixiao, inviting him to attend the Flower-Viewing and Fishing Banquet. Though Lin Zixiao's poetry and paintings fell short of making a resounding name across Lin'an, he still carved out a modest niche among the city's literary circles. And as the Prefect, Zhu Xiyan was also a man of letters who loved poetry and literature—otherwise, how could he have raised his daughter to become an accomplished female *ci* poet [TN: A poet specializing in the Song dynasty *ci* lyric form]?

林子孝接到请帖时，林觉正在一旁，便请族叔带他到赏花钓鱼宴上走一遭，体验一下文人风骚。林子孝说，像这种场合临安没有名头的文人根本没有机会参加，何况林觉只是个武夫。谁让林子孝有面子，能让知府大人瞧得上，带个把人去应该没问题。
When Lin Zixiao received the invitation, Lin Jue happened to be standing nearby. He simply asked his clan uncle to bring him along to the Flower-Viewing and Fishing Banquet for a look, to experience the literary refinements of scholars. Lin Zixiao pointed out that scholars without a reputation in Lin'an stood no chance of attending such an occasion, let alone a mere military man like Lin Jue. But since Lin Zixiao stood in high enough standing with the Prefect, bringing along an extra person or two shouldn't pose any problem.

就这样林觉参加了赏花钓鱼宴。这种宴会，除了赏花、钓鱼、品茶、听曲儿、宴饮外，最重要的就是赋诗作词，展示文人才华。
And so, Lin Jue attended the Flower-Viewing and Fishing Banquet. Beyond appreciating the flowers, fishing, tea tasting, listening to music, and feasting, the most crucial part of such banquets was composing *shi* and *ci* poetry to showcase literary talent.

林觉玩了一会儿，当文人们以“春”为主题赋诗时，他毫无兴致。直到朱淑真出现，他眼前一亮，被深深吸引，内心爱意萌生，平生对附庸风雅的文词诗句最是愚笨的他，竟然把朱淑真做的那首词记在心间，现在依然能吟诵而出:
Lin Jue idled about for a short while, but when the scholars began composing verses on the theme of "spring," he grew entirely disinterested. It wasn't until Zhu Shuzhen appeared that his eyes lit up. He was deeply captivated, love budding in his heart. Though he had been hopelessly inept at affecting literary airs his entire life, he actually memorized the lyric she composed, and could still recite it to this day:

迟迟春日弄轻柔，花径暗香流。清明过了，不堪回首，云锁朱楼。
午窗睡起莺声巧，何处唤春愁？绿杨影里，海棠亭畔，红杏梢头。（《眼儿媚·迟迟春日弄轻柔》）
> In late spring days the gentle breeze
> Plays with the slender willow trees;
> Fragrance permeates the flowery pathway.
> After the clear, bright Mourning Day,
> How can I bear to think of bygone hours
> Now clouds have veiled my crimson bower?
> I wake from nap to hear the happy orioles sing.
> Where will their songs evoke the grief of spring?
> In willows' shade,
> By balustrade
> Of the Crabapple Bower,
> Amid apricot flowers.
> —*) [TN: Translated by the renowned literary master Xu Yuanchong]

恐怕这会成为他一生唯一记住的一首词，这就是爱情的力量。
This would likely be the only poem he ever remembered in his entire life—such was the power of love.

只可惜娶了朱淑真后，曾经美好的想象在现实面前不堪一击。新婚之夜，她不愿圆房，甚至手拿剪刀做出不要命的样子。林觉爱着她，也就放弃了，想着刚成亲她不习惯，以后会慢慢接纳自己，毕竟两人已是夫妻。
Unfortunately, after marrying Zhu Shuzhen, those once-beautiful fantasies proved fragile in the face of reality. On their wedding night, she refused to consummate the marriage, even gripping a pair of scissors as if ready to throw her life away. Because Lin Jue loved her, he backed down, figuring she just wasn't used to it since they were newly wed. He believed she would slowly accept him in time, as they were officially husband and wife after all.

事实却相反，他的理解并未换来朱淑真的改变，她不仅不愿与他同桌吃饭，甚至连句话都不愿与他讲，整日不是在屋里写诗作词，就是出去约好友郊游玩乐，甚至还有文人才子在其中。
The reality, however, was the exact opposite. His understanding did not bring about any change in Zhu Shuzhen. Not only did she refuse to eat at the same table, she wouldn't even speak a single word to him. She spent her days either sequestered in her room composing poetry or venturing out on excursions with friends—young men of literary renown among them.

林觉再也不想忍下去。有一日，冲进竹兰居，逼迫朱淑真与他圆房。这女人力气不大，却懂得如何保护自己，一脚踢在林觉的要害处，他疼了好一会儿，一气之下开始打她。
Lin Jue could not bear it any longer. One day, he barged into the Zhulan Residence, forcing Zhu Shuzhen to consummate their marriage. The woman didn't have much physical strength, but she knew perfectly well how to protect herself. She landed a kick right in Lin Jue's groin, leaving him in agony for quite a while. In a fit of rage, he began to beat her.

被打后，她那个可怜样儿，让林觉既解气、兴奋，又找回丈夫的自尊，于是家暴朱淑真就成为林觉每隔一段时间要做的事情，尤其是想要与她圆房时。后来干脆将青楼女子带回家，当着她的面亲热，换来的依然是朱淑真的冷漠，这冷漠他看着更来气。
Seeing her wretched state after the beating gave Lin Jue a rush of satisfaction and excitement; it restored his masculine pride. Thus, committing domestic violence against Zhu Shuzhen became something he did regularly—especially whenever he wanted to sleep with her. Later, he outright brought women from the *qinglou* home, being intimate with them right in front of her. Yet, all it earned him was Zhu Shuzhen's ongoing indifference, an icy demeanor that only infuriated him more.

今日就是在这种情况下又打了她。没成想用的力气大了些，打着打着，一把推去，她撞到柱子上晕死过去。林觉还担心好一阵儿，毕竟打死人要偿命的。谢天谢地，终于醒来，可却变了个人，样貌身段还是朱淑真，其他完全不一样。
It was under exactly these circumstances that he had beaten her again today. He hadn't expected to use so much force. Mid-beating, he gave her a shove, and she slammed into a pillar, knocked out cold. Lin Jue had been genuinely worried for a good while, since killing someone meant paying with his own life. Thank heavens she eventually woke up—but she had turned into an entirely different person. Her face and figure were still Zhu Shuzhen's, but everything else was entirely distinct.

有一次朱淑真被打，仅仅提出和离，还被他一巴掌打回去，自此再也没提过。没想到眼前这个朱淑真竟然提出让休了她？林觉既诧异又满心不乐意。
Once before, after taking a beating, Zhu Shuzhen had merely suggested a mutual divorce—only for him to strike her down with a single slap. She never brought it up again. Who would have thought the Zhu Shuzhen standing before him now would actually demand that he issue a letter of divorce? Lin Jue was both bewildered and completely unwilling.

林觉又说出那句话：“你不是朱淑真。”
Lin Jue repeated that phrase once again: "You are not Zhu Shuzhen."

千里寻说：“你老强调这句话干嘛？难道真想让所有人知道朱淑真因你家暴而亡？让官府把你抓起来？”
Qianli Xun retorted, "Why do you keep emphasizing that? Do you truly want everyone to know that Zhu Shuzhen died from your domestic abuse? To have the local yamen arrest you?"

“官府不会抓我，因为所有人都能证明，你跟朱淑真长得一模一样。”
"The authorities won't arrest me, because everyone can testify that you are the spitting image of Zhu Shuzhen."

“那不就结了，我就是朱淑真，你还怀疑什么？”
"Well, that settles it then. I *am* Zhu Shuzhen. What are you still doubting?"

“她不会提出让我休了她。”
"She wouldn't have demanded that I divorce her."

千里寻找椅子坐下：“那是以前，现在她的想法已改变。”
Qianli Xun found a chair and sat down. "That was the past. Her mindset has changed now."

“难道你不知一个女人被丈夫休了是什么下场？”
"Do you truly not know the fate of a woman discarded by her husband?"

“不管是什么下场，总比和你一起过日子强。”
"Whatever the fate, it's bound to be better than spending my days with you."

“总之，我不同意！”
"In short, I absolutely refuse!"

林觉态度强硬，又怎能难倒千里寻：“你不同意，没关系，我把你天天关在家里，不给你吃，不给你喝，也不让你上茅房，屋子里也不放夜壶，关你十天十夜，看你同不同意。”
Lin Jue's stubborn stance stood no chance of deterring Qianli Xun. "It doesn't matter if you refuse. I'll just lock you in the house every single day. I won't give you anything to eat or drink, nor will I let you use the privy [TN: an outhouse or latrine], and I certainly won't leave a chamber pot in the room. I'll lock you up for ten days and ten nights and see if you still refuse then."

“你妄想！我是练兵官，天天要操练临安士兵，只要一天不去，就会有人来找我。”
"Keep dreaming! I am a Military Training Officer. I have to drill the Lin'an soldiers every day. If I'm absent for even a single day, someone will come looking for me."

“这个容易，大不了我让下人到你上班的地方……”
"That's easy. If it comes to that, I'll just have a servant go to your workplace..."

“上班？”
"Workplace?"

[TN: Qianli Xun inadvertently lets slip a modern term—"going to work"—that has no equivalent in Song Dynasty vocabulary.]

千里寻赶紧改口：“到你练兵的地方给你告个假，就说你病了，而且是很严重的病，需要在家待个十天半月，估计你的上司不会不同意。”
Qianli Xun hurriedly corrected herself: "Go to your training grounds to request a leave of absence for you. They'll say you've fallen ill, a very grave illness at that, requiring you to stay home for ten days to half a month. I doubt your commanding officer would refuse."

“你这个狠毒的女人！即便如此，林府的下人也不会随便被你差遣！”
"You vicious woman! Even so, the Lin household servants wouldn't be casually ordered around by you!"

“下人好说，使银子就行，有钱能使鬼推磨，再说刚才那两个下人可是听话得很，连银子都没使，乖乖把你关起来。”
"Servants are simple to manage; spending a few taels of silver is all it takes. Money makes the mare go. Besides, those two servants from earlier were exceptionally obedient; they locked you up without me even needing to spend a dime."

林觉气得不行：“这两个家伙！我迟早会把他们赶出去！”
Lin Jue was furious. "Those two scoundrels! I'll kick them out sooner or later!"

看这情况，眼前的朱淑真是铁了心要离开他。打又打不过，干脆服软：“跟你夫妻一场，也算缘分，我们好合好散，除被我休了这条路，还有第二条路，就是和离，到时你离开我传出去名声也好听些。”
Seeing the situation, it was clear the Zhu Shuzhen before him was dead set on leaving him. Moreover, he couldn't beat her in a fight, so he might as well yield. "We were once married, which is roughly a fate of its own. Let's part amicably. Besides dismissing you outright, there is another way—a mutual divorce. If you leave through that route, your good name will be much better preserved to outsiders."

千里寻并不了解林觉，毕竟刚认识他不久，但仅从打女人这一条判断，不是什么好东西，他的建议还需认真考量：“你能这么好？”
Qianli Xun didn't truly know Lin Jue—after all, she had only recently come to know him at all. But judging solely by the fact that he hit women, he was clearly no good. His suggestion merited careful scrutiny. "Could you be that kind?"

“你晕死后醒来，变成母夜叉，把你放在家里，我会寝食难安，早些把你安顿走，我也好再娶一位娘子过舒坦日子。不过，和离之事我们两个说了不算，必须将你的父母以及有身份的长辈请来，当然林家也会叫来长辈，再把媒人请来，当着所有人的面，征得所有人同意，把财产分割清楚，在和离的文书上签字按下指印，再由媒婆呈送官府，你我的姻缘才能结束。”
"After waking up from your faint, you've turned into a vicious shrew. Keeping you around would give me no peace—day or night. Settling your departure early means I can take another wife and live a comfortable life. However, a mutual divorce isn't something the two of us can just declare. We must invite your parents and senior family figures of status. Naturally, the Lin family will also call upon our elders, and we must hire a matchmaker. In front of everyone, we need to obtain broad consent, clearly execute the division of property, sign our names and affix our thumbprints on the mutual divorce deed. Only when the matchmaker submits it to the local yamen will our marital ties officially end."

千里寻心想，古代离个婚怎么这么麻烦，还得长辈同意。既然已穿越成古人，还是入乡随俗吧。
*Divorcing in ancient times is so needlessly complicated,* Qianli Xun thought to herself. *You actually need the elders' consent. But since I've already transmigrated as an ancient person, I might as well do as the Romans do.*

“我同意，那就和离。”
"I agree, let's proceed with a mutual divorce then."

“能不能看在我出了这么好的主意，帮我松开绳子？”
"Could you untie these ropes for me, considering I offered such an excellent idea?"

千里寻起身，拿起旁边的布子，走到床前，狠劲儿塞进林觉的嘴里。
Qianli Xun stood up, picked up the cloth nearby, strode to the bed, and forcefully jammed it into Lin Jue's mouth.

林觉怎么反抗都没用。
No matter how hard Lin Jue struggled, it was utterly useless.

千里寻指着他说：“本来我想关你好几日，看在你出了这么好一个主意，给你把时间缩短一下，只关你半天一晚上，明日一早再放你出去，这是对你殴打朱淑真的惩罚。”
Qianli Xun pointed a finger at him. "Originally, I intended to lock you up for a few days. But since you provided such a great idea, I'll shorten the duration. I'm only locking you up for half a day and tonight, and I'll let you out first thing tomorrow morning. Consider this your punishment for beating Zhu Shuzhen."

千里寻说完，转身离开。
Having said her piece, she turned and left.

林觉的嘴被堵着，不能大骂她，只能心里骂道：“走着瞧！你以为和离那么容易，到时长辈们的唾沫星子都能把你淹死，还有你爹娘，他们未必同意！哼！到时你就知道想离开林家没那么容易！”
With his mouth gagged, Lin Jue couldn't curse her aloud, so he could only swear internally. *Just you wait! Do you really think a mutual divorce is that easy? The spit from the elders will be enough to drown you, not to mention your own parents might not even agree! Hmph! You'll soon realize that leaving the Lin family won't be that simple!*

千里寻回到竹兰居，苦荞刚好将茶做好，还热着，见小姐回来，将茶盏端上：“小姐，喝茶。”
Qianli Xun returned to the Zhulan Residence. Ku Qiao had exactly finished preparing the tea, which was still hot. Seeing the Miss return, she presented the teacup, "Please have some tea, Miss."

千里寻接过，看到茶汤色泽鲜白，还有一层细腻的泡沫，跟她喝过的茶很不一样：“这是茶吗？”
Qianli Xun accepted it. Observing its bright white hue, topped with a fine, silky layer of froth, she found it entirely different from any tea she had tasted before. "Is this tea?"

“是我刚刚为小姐点的茶。”
"It is the whisked tea I just prepared for you, Miss."

“点茶？”千里寻听过点茶，第一次见，只知这茶做起来不简单，她端起茶盏，一口气喝下，口感绵密细腻，甘甜润滑，“不错！没想到你这丫头竟然会点茶。”
"Whisked tea?" Qianli Xun had heard of the practice but had never seen it before. She only knew the process was far from simple. Lifting the teacup, she downed it in one gulp. The mouthfeel was velvety and dense, with a lingering sweetness that coated the palate. "Not bad! I never expected a young maid like you to know how to whisk tea."

苦荞接过茶盏：“是小姐您以前教我的，您喜欢喝点的茶，又不想总自己点，就教会我，要不像点茶这种功夫，我作为下人是没机会学的。”
Ku Qiao took back the teacup. "I have only learned it thanks to you, Miss. You enjoyed drinking whisked tea but didn't always want to prepare it yourself, so you instructed me. Otherwise, as a mere servant, I wouldn't have possessed the opportunity to learn such a refined art."

“原来是我教你的？看来以后得倒过来，换你教我。”千里寻说完，到处找床，她太累了，想躺一会儿。
"So I was the one who taught you? It looks like we'll have to reverse the roles from now on; you'll have to teach me." Saying this, Qianli Xun looked around for a bed. She was exhausted and wanted to lie down for a while.

苦荞放下茶盏：“小姐，跟我来。”
Setting down the teacup, Ku Qiao said, "Miss, follow me."

千里寻跟着苦荞向里面拐去，一张闺床出现在眼前，她二话不说，躺了上去。
Qianli Xun followed Ku Qiao as she turned inward, where a maiden's bed appeared before them. Saying nothing, she immediately lay down on it.

苦荞走到床边：“小姐，宽了衣裳您再睡。”
Ku Qiao walked over to the bedside. "Miss, please undo your garments before sleeping."

千里寻坐起来，准备自己动手脱掉外衣，刚穿越过来，一切还不习惯。
Qianli Xun sat up, preparing to take off her outer robes herself. Having just arrived in this era, she still wasn't used to much of anything yet.

“小姐，您下床站着，我帮您脱。”
"Miss, please step out of bed and stand so I can help you disrobe."

千里寻照做。在苦荞帮她宽衣之际，问道：“为什么点茶这种功夫下人没机会学？”
Qianli Xun obliged. While Ku Qiao was helping her disrobe, she asked, "Why do servants lack the opportunity to learn the art of tea whisking?"

苦荞边帮小姐宽衣边说：“点茶的功夫是像小姐这样的千金小姐，以及豪门贵族人士必备的技能之一，也只有像小姐这样的人才有闲情逸致在一盏茶上用功夫。市井小民，还有我们这些下人，既没闲情逸致，也没那机会，我是沾了小姐的光。”
As she helped the Miss undress, Ku Qiao explained, "The art of tea whisking is a requisite skill for noble daughters like yourself, as well as wealthy and prominent families. Only people of your status have the leisure and the graceful inclination to dedicate so much effort to a single cup of tea. Commoners in the marketplace, and servants like us, possess neither the leisure time nor the opportunity. I only profited from your grace, Miss."

外衣脱掉后，千里寻一身轻松，穿着中衣躺入被中：“我太累了，天没黑就睡，等天黑了也不要叫我，我要一直睡到明天天亮。”
Once her outer garments were off, Qianli Xun felt a wave of relief. Clad only in her inner robes, she slipped under the covers. "I'm much too tired. I'm going to sleep before the sun even sets. When it gets dark, don't wake me. I plan to sleep straight through until dawn."

说完没多久，千里寻已进入梦乡。
Not long after saying this, Qianli Xun drifted into dreamland.

苦荞放下床幔，轻轻走出去，把门带好。
Ku Qiao lowered the bed curtains, stepped out softly, and tightly closed the door behind her.
